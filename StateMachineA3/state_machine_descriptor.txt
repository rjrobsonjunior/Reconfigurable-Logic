Inputs: LeftSensor, RightSensor, Enable, Losting
Outputs: MotorSpeedLeft, MotorSpeedRight, Looking

MotorSpeed: 00 = stopped, 01 = half, 10 = full

States: 
        S0 (Idle      ) [00, 00]
        S1 (Full Speed) [10, 10]
        S2 (Turn Left ) [01, 10]
        S3 (Turn Right) [10, 01]
        S4 (Seek Left ) [00, 01]
        S5 (Seek Right) [01, 00]
        S6 (Lost      ) [00, 00]
